Courses we offer

Aurora University offers a diverse range of courses spanning disciplines such as business management, psychology, literature, computer science, economics, languages, sociology, anatomy, marketing, environmental science, political science, criminal justice, education, graphic design, and engineering principles.

Buisness Managment

The business management course at Aurora University provides students with essential knowledge and skills in organizational behavior, strategic planning, and leadership, preparing them for success in diverse corporate environments.

Computer Science

The computer science course at Aurora University introduces students to fundamental concepts and skills in programming, algorithms, and problem-solving, equipping them with the knowledge to thrive in a technology-driven world.

Sociology

The sociology course at Aurora University explores the study of society, culture, and human behavior, analyzing social structures, inequalities, and interactions to foster a deeper understanding of the world around us.

Our Facilities

Aurora University offers modern and comprehensive facilities to support student learning and engagement. Our campus features state-of-the-art classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a library with extensive resources, recreational spaces, athletic facilities, student lounges, and dining options. These facilities are designed to enhance the academic experience and promote a vibrant campus community.

World Class Library

The library at Aurora University provides a rich resource for academic study and research, offering a vast collection of books, journals, online databases, and multimedia resources. It also provides quiet study areas, collaborative spaces, and expert assistance from librarians to support students in their academic pursuits.

Largest Play Ground

Aurora University offers diverse sports activities catering to various interests and skill levels. Students can participate in intercollegiate athletics, intramural sports leagues, fitness classes, and recreational programs. Our sports facilities include gymnasiums, fitness centers, outdoor fields, and courts, providing opportunities for physical activity, teamwork, and personal wellness.

Tasty and Healthy Food

The cafeteria at Aurora University offers a variety of freshly prepared meals and snacks in a welcoming dining environment. Students can enjoy diverse menu options, including hot meals, salads, sandwiches, and desserts. The cafeteria provides a central gathering space for students to dine, socialize, and relax between classes.
